Laboratory Reservation and Management System

The Laboratory Reservation and Utilization System for Pamantasan ng Cabuyao (PNC) is a web-based platform designed to streamline the scheduling, management, and monitoring of laboratory room usage across the institution.

The system enables students and faculty members to reserve laboratory rooms by checking real-time availability, submitting booking requests, and tracking reservation status. It ensures efficient allocation of laboratory resources by preventing schedule conflicts, enforcing operating hours (7:00 AM to 10:00 PM), and providing immediate feedback on availability.

For administrators and laboratory coordinators, the system provides a centralized dashboard to review, approve, or reject reservation requests, monitor scheduling conflicts, and analyze laboratory utilization through visual reports and trends. The platform supports data-driven decision-making by highlighting peak usage hours, frequently used laboratories, and overall reservation statistics.

Overall, the system improves operational efficiency, reduces manual scheduling errors, and enhances accessibility and transparency in laboratory resource management at PNC.
